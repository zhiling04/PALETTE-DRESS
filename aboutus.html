Company Objectives

Provide a Diverse Collection

Offer a wide and varied selection of theme-based clothing options to cater to the diverse preferences and needs of our customers.


Cater to All Occasions

Ensure that Palette becomes the go-to destination for customers seeking clothing for various occasions, including casual outings, special events, themed parties, and more.


Enhance Customer Experience

Continuously improve the overall customer experience by offering convenient rental processes, excellent customer service, and easy returns, fostering loyalty and positive word-of-mouth.


Educate on Sustainable Fashion

Educate customers about the benefits of sustainable fashion and the positive impact of renting clothing, encouraging a shift towards more conscious consumer habits.



Company❜s Background

In the year 2024, Syaf, Kai Yi, Yulu, and Wan Xuan came together to establish Palette. The inception of the company can be traced back to their first semester at Kolej Matrikulasi when they embarked on a video resume assignment for their Pengajian Am course. Little did they know that this assignment would spark the inspiration for a venture that would address a common challenge faced by themselves and their peers. As they delved into the world of video production, the founders encountered a significant hurdle - the struggle to find suitable clothing for their shoots. This dilemma was not unique to them; it resonated with many of their peers who faced similar issues when trying to source appropriate attire for various occasions. The result was a collective effort to borrow clothes from friends and roommates, highlighting the lack of accessible and convenient options for acquiring suitable clothing. Recognizing the widespread nature of this challenge, Syaf, Kai Yi, Yulu, and Wan Xuan saw an opportunity to create a solution. This shared experience became the driving force behind the establishment of Palette. The company's mission became clear: to provide a platform where individuals could easily access a diverse range of clothing options for various occasions, eliminating the need to borrow from others or compromise on their personal style (Priem., et al, 2013).
